Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

Summary: Workout Planner is a client-side web application that helps you view and organize your workout plans from Google Sheets. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any servers. All data stays on your device.

1. Information We Access

Workout Planner requires access to:

  • Google Account Authentication: To verify your identity and access your Google Sheets
  • Google Sheets Data: Read-only access to specific Google Sheets you choose to open

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Authenticating your Google account
  • Reading workout data from your selected Google Sheets
  • Displaying your workout plans in an organized calendar interface
  • Storing your preferences locally on your device (sheet history, authentication tokens)

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage:

  • Authentication Tokens: Stored to maintain your login session
  • Sheet History: Sheet IDs, titles, and access timestamps for quick access
  • User Preferences: Selected dates and view modes

Important: No data is transmitted to or stored on our servers. Everything remains on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

Workout Planner uses the following Google services:

  • Google OAuth 2.0: For secure authentication
  • Google Sheets API: For read-only access to your workout data

These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • All connections use HTTPS encryption
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely in your browser
  • We only request read-only access to your Google Sheets
  • No server-side data storage or processing

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible in your browser's localStorage
  • Deletion: Clear your browser's localStorage or use the "Delete" buttons in the app to remove sheet history
  • Revoke Access: Disconnect the app from your Google account at any time via Google Account Permissions

7. Cookies and Tracking

Workout Planner does not use cookies or any tracking mechanisms. We do not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking tools.

8. Children's Privacy

Workout Planner does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this application without parental consent.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

10. Open Source

Workout Planner is open source. You can review the complete source code and verify our privacy claims on GitHub.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the application, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
